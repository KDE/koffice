3.1. Can KChart accept text files of data, such as columns of numbers, for plotting?
3.2. Why do some of the commands from the Edit Configure... menu not function?
3.1.

Can KChart accept text files of data, such as columns of numbers, for plotting?

No. At this time KChart can only make graphs from inside KSpread.

3.2.

Why do some of the commands from the Edit Configure... menu not function?

KChart is still under development and some features do not work.
