KPresenter Help
 
 

The Insert Menu KPresenter

Page...

To insert a new page choose this command. You are then presented with the Insert Page dialog.

Picture...

The file open dialog will be opened and enable you to choose a picture that will be inserted in your active document. KPresenter is able to read different graphical formats.

Clipart...

You can choose a clipart in WMF format.
